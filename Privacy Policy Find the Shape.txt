PRIVACY POLICY — Find The Shape!
Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Find The Shape! ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application Find The Shape! (the "App").
By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
Information collected automatically:

Device type and operating system version
App usage data (levels completed, session duration)
Advertising ID (used for personalized ads)
Crash reports and performance data

Information we do NOT collect:

Your name, email, or personal identity
Location data
Photos, camera, or microphone access
Payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)


3. How We Use Information
We use collected data to:

Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
Improve app performance and fix bugs
Analyze gameplay to improve difficulty balancing
Track daily streaks and game progress (stored locally on your device)


4. Advertising
Find The Shape! uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including:

Banner ads
Interstitial ads
Rewarded video ads

Google AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: g.co/adsettings
For more information on how Google uses data: policies.google.com/privacy

5. In-App Purchases
Find The Shape! offers optional in-app purchases (coins, power-ups, remove ads). All purchases are:

Processed securely through Google Play Billing
Non-refundable unless required by applicable law
Not shared with third parties

We do not store or have access to your payment information.

6. Data Storage
Game progress (level, coins, streak, owned items) is stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data:

Is not uploaded to our servers
Is deleted if you uninstall the app
Is not shared with third parties


7. Children's Privacy
Find The Shape! is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
If the app is used by children under 13, personalized ads will not be shown in compliance with COPPA regulations.
